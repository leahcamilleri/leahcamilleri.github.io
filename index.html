Roles in Animation Production




There are many different roles in the animation production for the finished work and every role carries out a uniquely imporant function.

These roles are split into three different catagories, being Production Managment,Development, Pre-Production, Production, Post-Production and Distribution.

Some key players in the animation field right now are Dreamworks, Studio Ghibli and Disney which are all companies that have different people doing these select roles.
